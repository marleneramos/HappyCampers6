It rained for days and days. But yesterday it wasn’t rainy. It was a bright and sunny day, so Robert went to the park with his friends. It was chilly and windy so he wore his warm jacket. One of Robert’s friends had a kite. It was a good day to fly a kite! But suddenly the wind blew the kite into a tree. Robert climbed the tree to get the kite. But the branch wasn’t strong and he fell into a mud puddle! “Are you OK?” everyone shouted.
“Yes, but I’m cold and wet. I want to go home!”

They all went back to Robert’s house. Robert looked cold and wet. Robert’s mother made a hot drink for everyone. Now everyone was happy again!
