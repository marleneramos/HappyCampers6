GRAMMAR PASS

  • Who did you go with?

  • When did you go?

  • What did you see?

  • Where did you go?

  1.      did you go? Movie City.

  2.      did you eat? Pizza.

  3.      did you go with? David.

  4.      did you go? Yesterday.

  5. We use Where, What, When, Who +      in questions about the past.
