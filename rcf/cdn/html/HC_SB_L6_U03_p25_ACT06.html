GRAMMAR PASS

  • I didn’t want to be late.

  • I didn’t take my wallet.

  1. I didn’t have    had     my books at school.

  2. She didn’t watched    watch     the movie.

  3. He didn’t listen    listened     to the song.

  4. Use didn’t + infinitive to talk about affirmative    negative     sentences in the past.
